Black Hole Discovery

Published: June 2026 • Reading Time: 5 Minutes

Scientists have recently discovered another supermassive black hole using advanced space telescopes. This discovery helps astronomers understand galaxy formation and the evolution of our universe.

🌌 What is a Black Hole?

A black hole is a region of spacetime where gravity is so strong that nothing, not even light, can escape.

🚀 Why is this Discovery Important?

It provides evidence about the early universe and helps scientists understand how galaxies evolve.
